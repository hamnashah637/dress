SHIPPING INFORMATION

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, customer will receive a verification code on the provided mobile phone number that is needed to be entered on the confirmation page to verify the order. If the verification code is not received, an automated call will be made from our end to verify provided contact details. Bonanza Satrangi reserves the right to not ship any Cash on Delivery (COD) order that is not verified by the customer via SMS OTP or automated call.

Domestic Delivery

Order deliveries may take up to 3-5 working days

During the Sale Period, orders may be delivered in 6-8 working days.

Flat Rs.100 shipping charges on all orders.

We may split your order (for local orders only), for smoother delivery process and you may receive your order in multiple packages. There will be no additional delivery charges on orders that are being split.

During festivals like Eid-ul-Fitr or Eid-ul-Adha order deliveries may take more time than usual. Please refer to the delivery timelines shared on the website homepage or contact us on our helpline at +92-21-111-244-266 for more information.

International Delivery

Order deliveries may take up to 8-12 working days.

During the Sale Period, Orders may be delivered in 12-15 working days.

Additional (if any) Custom Duty to be borne by the Customer at the time of delivery.

Any taxes or duty charges will be payable by the customer to the courier company at the time of the delivery.

During festivals like Eid-ul-Fitr or Eid-ul-Adha order deliveries may take more time than usual. Please refer to the delivery timelines shared on the website homepage or contact us on our helpline at +92-21-111-244-266 for more information.

Delivery charges

Charges for international orders are calculated based on the destination and the weight of your shipment. Duties and taxes are most typically not included in the price of the goods you purchase online, and might not be included in the overall shipping costs you pay. Also, please ensure to check the Duty charges (if any) of your shipment as the duty charges will be payable by the customer to the courier company at the time of the delivery.

Please note that international orders may be subject to taxes & duties and will be the responsibility of the customer. Any applicable duties or fees (If any) will be collected at the time of order delivery.

Failed to pay the duties and taxes will lead to order cancellation/ return, hence payment will not be refunded to the customer
